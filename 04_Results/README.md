# 📈 Results & Policy Output (`/04_Results`)

## 📌 Overview
This directory contains the final synthesis outputs of the Dhaka PM₂.₅ forecasting pipeline. It houses the legally grounded Environmental Action Advisory Briefs (EAABs) generated by the RAG-LLM agent, alongside the raw semantic evaluation diagnostics used to mathematically verify the system's factual accuracy.

By storing these scenario-specific results and logs separately from the execution code, this repository adheres to strict MLOps principles. It allows reviewers to independently audit the model's policy outputs and semantic verification scores without needing to re-run the entire inference pipeline.

## 📂 Scenario Outputs & Diagnostics
The outputs are categorized into three distinct meteorological and regulatory scenarios. Each scenario contains the human-readable Markdown policy brief and its corresponding RAGAS-Proxy mathematical evaluation log.

### 1️⃣ Winter Inversion Crisis (PURPLE Alert)
Driven by severe boundary-layer compression and thermal inversions, representing Dhaka's most dangerous exposure periods.
* **`EAAB_Winter_Inversion_Scenario.md`**: The generated policy directives. Includes the automated "Model Bias Warning" to computationally compensate for tree-based underprediction during extreme peaks.
* **`EAAB_eval_diagnostics.json`**: The raw evaluation log for this brief.

### 2️⃣ Monsoon Washout (AMBER Alert)
Driven by natural wet-scavenging and precipitation controls.
* **`EAAB_Monsoon_Scenario.md`**: The generated policy directives highlighting routine monitoring and washout effects.
* **`EAAB_Monsoon_eval_diagnostics.json`**: The raw evaluation log for this brief.

### 3️⃣ Extreme Episode Ceiling (PURPLE Alert)
A peak extreme pollution event testing the ceiling limits of the National Committee on Air Pollution Control (NCAPC) emergency protocols.
* **`EAAB_Extreme_Scenario.md`**: The generated policy directives invoking strict municipal shutdowns based on APCR 2022 Rule 15 mandates.
* **`EAAB_Extreme_eval_diagnostics.json`**: The raw evaluation log for this brief.

## ⚖️ Understanding the Diagnostic Logs (`.json` files)
The `.json` files contain the unit-by-unit semantic evaluation data generated by the local RAGAS-Proxy framework. They serve as the irrefutable "receipts" that validate the agent's performance.

**Key Metrics Tracked in Logs:**
* **Faithfulness (Semantic):** Mean cosine similarity evaluating whether generated answer units are strictly grounded in retrieved policy chunks.
* **Answer Relevance:** Evaluates the semantic alignment between the generated answer and the original composite query.
* **Context Precision:** Measures the proportion of retrieved documents that were successfully cited in the generated EAAB.
* **Hallucination Rate:** Validates the proportion of answer units failing to match retrieved context, proving the 0.0% hallucination rate.
